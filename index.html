The Lagoon

The Lagoon is a student service group looking to create more financial resources for Clark College students that may be struggling to keep up with the costs of their classes.

Financial Struggles

Of all Washington residents, there are over 750 thousand, nearly 10% of the total population, who fall below the poverty level[15]. There are also around 790 thousand residents that have some amount of student loan debt, 21.4% of them owing $20,000 to $40,000[9]. Being in debt is already hard enough, and for anyone who is already struggling financially, it can feel like drowning. That kind of stress can make it so much more difficult to get through classwork and focus on your long term goals, and it is a heavy burden to carry. Luckily, there are many resources available to help Clark College students make that burden a little lighter.
